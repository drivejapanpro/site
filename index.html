Your Guide to Driving in Japan

The best app to help you prepare for the Japanese driving license exam.

Drive Japan Pro is an educational app designed to help expats, tourists, and professionals pass the Japanese driver’s license written exams, including the learner’s permit, full permit, and foreign license conversion tests.

Features

Practice Tests

Hundreds of practice questions to get you ready for the exam.

Flashcards

Memorize important signs and rules with our flashcards.

Signs and Markings

A complete guide to all Japanese road signs and markings.

Driving Course

Learn the basics of driving in Japan.

Moped (Gentsuki) Test

Specific practice tests for the moped license.
